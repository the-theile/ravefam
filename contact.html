Common questions

How do I delete my account? ▼
Email us at bump@myravefam.com with the subject "Delete my account" and we'll process your request within 30 days, including removal of all associated data.
I forgot my password — what do I do? ▼
On the login screen, tap "Forgot password" and enter your email. You'll receive a reset link within a few minutes. Check your spam folder if it doesn't arrive.
How do I leave or remove a crew? ▼
Open the crew in the app, tap the settings icon, and select "Leave Crew." If you're the Crew Lead, you'll need to transfer leadership or disband the crew first.
Is RaveFAM free to use? ▼
Yes — RaveFAM is free to sign up and use. No credit card required. We may introduce optional premium features in the future, but the core experience will always be free.
I found a security vulnerability. Who do I tell? ▼
Please email us immediately at bump@myravefam.com with the subject "Security Issue." We take all security reports seriously and will respond as fast as possible.
