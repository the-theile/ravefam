Common questions

How do I delete my account? ▼
Email us at bump@myravefam.com with the subject "Delete my account" and we'll process your request within 30 days, including removal of all associated data.
I forgot my password — what do I do? ▼
On the login screen, tap "Forgot password" and enter your email. You'll receive a reset link within a few minutes. Check your spam folder if it doesn't arrive.
How do I join a crew? ▼
Ask your crew leader to switch their crew to Recruiting mode, then scan their QR code or enter the invite code they share with you. Once you claim your spot, you'll have full access to the crew.
Can I be in multiple crews? ▼
Yes — there's no limit. You can be a member of as many crews as you like, and lead more than one if you want.
What's the difference between Secret, Recruiting, and Locked In? ▼
Your crew has three modes. Secret is the default — only the leader can see it, full control, no one else involved. Switch to Recruiting when you're ready to invite people in — you'll get a QR code and invite link. Flip to Locked In when your circle is complete — no new members can join, and your existing crew gets a heads-up notification. You can switch back to Recruiting any time if you need to add someone.
How do I leave or remove a crew? ▼
Open the crew in the app, tap the settings icon, and select "Leave Crew." If you're the Crew Lead, leadership transfer is on the way — for now, email us at bump@myravefam.com and we'll sort it out.
A crew leader added me before I signed up — how do I get removed? ▼
Email us at bump@myravefam.com with the subject "Remove my profile" and we'll delete it within 30 days — no questions asked.
Is RaveFAM free to use? ▼
Yes — RaveFAM is free to sign up and use. No credit card required. We may introduce optional premium features in the future, but the core experience will always be free.
I found a security vulnerability. Who do I tell? ▼
Please email us immediately at bump@myravefam.com with the subject "Security Issue." We take all security reports seriously and will respond as fast as possible.
