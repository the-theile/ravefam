Everything you need

Built for how
crews actually work

One home for your crews. See who's going. Share memories. Never lose the group in the crowd.

👑
Crew management
Create crews, set them to Recruiting or Locked In, and control exactly who's in. Your inner circle stays yours.
🎵
Rich member profiles
Fav artists, genres, vibe tags, and private notes — yours to share, theirs to see. Know your tribe before the gates open.
📲
QR invite codes
One scan or a 6-digit code and you're in instantly. Share at the festival, drop it in a story, or paste it in the group chat.
🎟️
See who's going
Browse what your crew has added, mark yourself Going or Interested, and see exactly who from your tribe is heading to each event.
⏳
Live countdowns
Days, hours, minutes — the hype is real. Every crew card and festival shows exactly how long until the next one drops.
✨
Profile claiming
Crew leaders can add members before they sign up — they appear as unclaimed. When they claim their profile they unlock crew features like Dream Board and member connections.
