How PLUR Points Work
Real activity, verified by the app — not something you can fake. Here's what earns each track.
☮️
Peace
Personal presence, prep & safety
  • Complete your profile
  • Attend a rave solo
  • Check in with Huddle Beacon at a rave
  • RSVP "Going" early for an upcoming fest
  • Keep your profile fresh over time
💗
Love
Shared memories & supporting your crew
  • Rave together with a crewmate
  • Add photos/videos to the crew archive
  • Drop a playlist for the crew
  • Hype someone's Dream Board pin
  • Add to a crew's shared Game Plan
  • Write a "how we met" story
✊
Unity
Growing & aligning the fam
  • Someone claims your invite
  • Create or join a crew
  • Run a poll the whole crew votes on
  • Vote on every poll in your crew
  • Fill every open slot in a crew
  • Rave with people from more than one of your crews
🙏
Respect
Stepping up for the community
  • Leave a vendor review
  • Post a "spotted right now" vendor tip
  • Take a safety role in a Game Plan (Driver, First Aid...)
  • Stick around — a small loyalty bonus over time
  • Earn a FAM Pin
🛡️
Points are calculated and verified by RaveFam itself — never something your own app can set. This keeps the leaderboard fair for everyone in the fam.
