Design Review

PLUR Points — 13 Mockups

Directions for presenting PLUR Points (Peace / Love / Unity / Respect) — a fun, verified rewards system for profile completion, raving with your crew, growing the fam, and community respect. Each mockup is a clickable static using the app's real design tokens. See the plan doc for the full activity list, data model, and anti-abuse design behind these.

Round 1 — initial exploration
Mockup 1
Deprioritized
PLUR Ring
Compact 4-segment dial wrapped around the avatar. Feedback: clashes with the profile's existing rainbow avatar-ring border — kept for reference, superseded by Round 2.
Mockup 2
Stat Block Grid
2x2 grid of big neon numbers per letter, styled to match the app's existing "Wrapped" year-in-review stat blocks — least new CSS, most native feel.
Mockup 3
Segmented Bar + Feed
Thin 4-color progress bar under the name, expands into a breakdown plus a recent-activity feed — doubles as the personal points ledger.
Mockup 4
Refined → Mockup 10
Leaderboard
Crew-scoped ranking by default, with a locked "Global" tab previewing the later opt-in worldwide leaderboard. Standalone page — superseded by embedding it in the real Stats page.
Mockup 5
"How Points Work" Info Sheet
Opened by tapping any profile's PLUR points — explains every way to earn each track, plus a personal-ledger link that only shows on your own profile.
Round 2 — placement options, recreated on the real hero card
Mockup 6
Not chosen
Banner Corner Badge
A small frosted pill in the currently-empty top-right corner of the gradient banner. Most understated — doesn't touch the name/avatar layout at all.
Mockup 7
Refined → Mockup 9
Slim Bar Under the Name
Liked the bar itself — feedback was to label it "PLUR" and move it below the whole hero block instead of squeezing it into the name column. See Mockup 9.
Mockup 8
Refined → Mockup 11
Compact Stat Strip
A single pill-shaped row below the action buttons showing all 4 numbers with emoji — liked this density. Its one-row, per-track-emoji style became the basis for Mockup 11.
Round 3 — placement finalized
Mockup 9
Refined → Mockup 11
PLUR Bar — Final Placement
Nailed the placement (full-width, above the buttons, "PLUR" label) but the two-line label+bar+expand felt bulkier than it needed to be — see Mockup 11 for the compact version.
Mockup 10
✓ Chosen — leaderboard
PLUR Leaderboard, in Crew Stats
Recreated against the real Stats page — a second "🏆 PLUR Leaderboard" section added below the existing Rave Leaderboard inside the Crew Stats subtab, reusing its exact row styling.
Round 4 — chosen direction, profile
Mockup 11
✓ Chosen — profile
PLUR Bar — Compact, One Row, Emojis
Mockup 8's compact emoji-per-track density (☮️💗✊🙏) in Mockup 9's placement (full-width, "PLUR" labeled, above the action buttons) — one dense row, all 4 numbers visible without expanding.
Round 5 — engagement concepts, chosen
Mockup 12
✓ Chosen — milestones
"My PLUR Milestones"
Cosmetic tier badges per track (4 tiers, apex always "[Track] Incarnate"), reusing the FAM Pins circular-glow badge language. Opens when you tap your own PLUR bar. Still no functional unlocks — just periodic "level up" payoffs.
Mockup 13
✓ Chosen — celebration
Milestone Unlocked — Celebration
A full-screen confetti moment for you (reusing the app's real claim-success pattern) plus a crew-feed post everyone sees (reusing `.achievement-feed-card` verbatim). Fires only on tier crossings, not every point.
