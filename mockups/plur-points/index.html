Design Review

PLUR Points — 5 Mockups

Five directions for presenting PLUR Points (Peace / Love / Unity / Respect) — a fun, verified rewards system for profile completion, raving with your crew, growing the fam, and community respect. Each mockup is a clickable static using the app's real design tokens. See the plan doc for the full activity list, data model, and anti-abuse design behind these.

Mockup 1
PLUR Ring
Compact 4-segment dial wrapped around the avatar — the smallest footprint, works on any profile card, expands to a breakdown on tap.
Mockup 2
Stat Block Grid
2x2 grid of big neon numbers per letter, styled to match the app's existing "Wrapped" year-in-review stat blocks — least new CSS, most native feel.
Mockup 3
Segmented Bar + Feed
Thin 4-color progress bar under the name, expands into a breakdown plus a recent-activity feed — doubles as the personal points ledger.
Mockup 4
Leaderboard
Crew-scoped ranking by default, with a locked "Global" tab previewing the later opt-in worldwide leaderboard.
Mockup 5
"How Points Work" Info Sheet
Opened by tapping any profile's PLUR points — explains every way to earn each track, plus a personal-ledger link that only shows on your own profile.
