RaveFAM is built for the rave community, by the rave community — based in Florida, USA. We built this app to make your crew life better, not to harvest your data. This policy tells you exactly what we collect, why we collect it, and how to get it deleted. By using RaveFAM at myravefam.com, you agree to what's described here.

01 Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, including:

  • Account information: Your name, email address, and password when you create an account.
  • Profile information: Your raver handle, music genre preferences, vibe tags, home city, Instagram handle, and phone number (optional).
  • Crew & event data: Crew names, crew status (Secret, Recruiting, or Locked In), rave and festival events you track, attendance status, Dream Board wish lists, and invite codes you generate or use.
  • Photos: A single photo you choose to share with each crew member connection, stored to enable the member connection feature.
  • "How We Met" stories: Personal text you write about how you met another crew member. Visible to both parties in the connection.
  • Private notes: Notes a crew leader writes about individual members. These are visible only to the leader — the member the note is about cannot see them.
  • Waitlist information: If you join our waitlist, we collect your name, Instagram handle, and email address.
  • Usage data: Pages visited, features used, and interactions within the app to help us improve the service.
  • Device information: Browser type, operating system, and IP address for security and analytics purposes.

02 Unclaimed Member Profiles

RaveFAM allows crew leaders to add members to their crew before those members sign up. This means we may hold profile data — including a name, vibe tags, favourite artists, genres, and events — about a person who has not yet created a RaveFAM account or agreed to these terms.

Here is how this works in practice:

  • Unclaimed profiles are visible only to the crew leader until the crew switches to Recruiting.
  • You can claim your profile at any time by signing up and using the invite code or QR code shared by the leader.
  • If you were added as an unclaimed member and never wish to sign up, you can request removal by emailing bump@myravefam.com — we will delete your profile within 30 days.
  • Crew leaders are responsible for only adding people they have a genuine relationship with and who would reasonably expect to be included.

If someone added you to a crew without your knowledge and you want your data removed, just email bump@myravefam.com. We'll sort it promptly.

03 How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Create and manage your RaveFAM account
  • Enable crew creation, invitations, and event tracking features
  • Display your profile to crew members you are connected with
  • Store and display "How We Met" stories and member photos between connected crew members
  • Send transactional emails (account verification, password resets)
  • Send waitlist notification emails when the app launches or updates
  • Improve and develop new features based on usage patterns
  • Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, or security incidents
  • Comply with applicable legal obligations

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We do not use your data for targeted advertising.

04 Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a managed database platform that provides industry-standard encryption at rest and in transit (TLS/SSL). Our servers are located in the United States.

We implement reasonable technical and organizational security measures to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

You are responsible for keeping your account password confidential. Please notify us immediately at bump@myravefam.com if you suspect unauthorized access to your account.

05 Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or rent your personal data. We may share your information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • With crew members: Your profile name, handle, vibe tags, and other profile information are visible to crew members you are connected with. Visibility depends on crew status — in Secret mode, only the crew leader can see the crew. When a crew switches to Recruiting or Locked In, active members can see each other's profiles. Private notes a leader writes about you are never visible to you — only to the leader who wrote them.
  • Service providers: We use the following third-party services to operate RaveFAM:
    • Supabase — database hosting and authentication
    • Vercel — website hosting and usage analytics (via Vercel Insights)
    • Formsubmit.co — waitlist notification emails
    These providers are contractually bound to protect your data and use it only to provide services to us.
  • Legal requirements: If required by law, court order, or governmental authority, we may disclose your information.
  • Business transfers: If RaveFAM is acquired or merges with another company, your data may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will notify you beforehand.

06 Cookies & Tracking

RaveFAM uses essential cookies and local storage to keep you logged in and maintain your session.

We use Vercel Insights for basic, privacy-friendly analytics (page views, feature usage) to understand how people use the app. Vercel Insights does not use tracking cookies and does not identify individual users or track them across other sites.

We do not use third-party advertising cookies or cross-site tracking. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though this may affect app functionality.

07 Your Rights & Choices

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information via your account profile settings.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and associated data by emailing us at bump@myravefam.com. We will process requests within 30 days.
  • Portability: Request a copy of your data in a portable format.
  • Opt-out of emails: Unsubscribe from non-essential emails via the unsubscribe link in any email we send.

To exercise any of these rights, just email us at bump@myravefam.com. We actually respond.

08 Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the service. When you request account deletion:

  • Your account and profile data are deleted within 30 days.
  • Crew data, events, and Dream Board entries associated with your profile are also removed.
  • If you were a crew leader, your crew and any unclaimed member profiles within it are deleted.
  • Waitlist data is retained until you request removal or we determine it is no longer needed.

We may retain anonymized, aggregated usage data for analytics purposes after your account is deleted.

09 Age Requirement

RaveFAM is a platform built around electronic music events, which are typically restricted to adults aged 18 and over. This app is intended solely for users aged 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18.

If we become aware that someone under 18 has created an account, we will delete it promptly. If you believe someone under 18 has signed up, please contact us at bump@myravefam.com.

10 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. For significant changes, we will notify you via email or a prominent notice within the app. Your continued use of RaveFAM after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
